Cha Cha Ekes Husband, Austin Faani Makes Cryptic Post Hours After Public Reunion With Wife

February 29, 2024
Post

Austin Faani, the husband of popular Nollywood actress, Cha Cha Eke has shared a cryptic post on social media.

Naija News recalls that Cha Cha who had announced the end of her marriage some months ago due to domestic violence stormed a church in Asaba with her husband.

She spoke on her mental illness which has taken a toll on her marriage.

The thespian further narrated how she ‘went mad’ on an occasion but her husband supported her.

Speaking via Twitter hours after the reunion video went viral online, Faani wrote, “No weapon formed against me shall prosper. What if you formed the weapon by yourself, against yourself using the POWER OF YOUR MIND wrongly? Will it prosper or will it fail?”

In response to his question, cybercitizens gave different answers and opinions.
