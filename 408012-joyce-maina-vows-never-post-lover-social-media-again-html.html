- TV host Joyce Maina has vowed she will never again share her man on social media

TV host Joyce Maina has disclosed that she will never post a picture of anything about her next man.

This announcement comes a few days after her ex-fiance, Tony Kwalanda, was spotted on social media getting cosy with a new woman.

While on her Chat Spot show, Maina said she fears how Kenyans are fast at finding out someone's new partner regardless of what some posts.

''I fear Kenyans, they will find out who he is. I am not even going to post a shoe because they will find out who's shoe it belongs to.
