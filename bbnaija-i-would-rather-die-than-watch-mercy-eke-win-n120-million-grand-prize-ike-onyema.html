BBNaija: 'I Would Rather Die Than Watch Mercy Eke Win N120 Million Grand Prize'

July 03, 2024
Post

Big Brother Naija ‘All-Stars’ Housemate, Ike Onyema, has vowed never to allow his ex-girlfriend, Mercy Eke, to win the N120 million grand prize of the season.

Naija News reports that the reality TV star made this known on Friday during a conversation with his felliw housemate, Doyin.

According to him, Mercy took many business deals from him outside the Biggie house despite winning the grand prize of the ‘PepperDem’ edition.

Ike further stressed that he would rather die than watch Mercy Eke win the grand prize of the current edition.

Doyin interrupted, saying he would not be the one to decide who wins the show, and Ike responded that he would do everything possible to sabotage Mercy’s game.
