BBNaija Housemate Reveals He Paid N3.1million To Board Private Jet From Abuja To Lagos

May 23, 2024
Post

The 2020 Big Brother Naija reality show is getting more interesting after one of the housemates, Kiddwaya disclosed that he paid $8000 to board a private jet from Abuja to Lagos for the show.

In a now-viral video on the microblogging site, the housemate while interacting with his fellow contestants said he spent the said amount to meet up with the official screening of the reality show.

Kiddwaya further revealed to Wathoni, Tolanibaj, and Nengi during the chat that the President of Madagascar, Andry Rajoelina, is a friend to his father.

Naija News understands that the money the housemate said he spent on the local trip, $8000 if converted to Nigerian Naira will amount to N3.1 million naira.

Reacting to Kiddwaya’s trip, Nengi said she would have joined him if she knew he was flying down to Lagos in a private jet. The housemate said he would have as well let her go with him if only he knew as he was all alone in the jet with a bottle of wine.
