Fani Kayode, Dino Melaye Meet At Abuja Weeks After Fighting Dirty On Social Media

March 06, 2024
Post

Spokesman of the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP) presidential campaign council, Dino Melaye, and former Minister of Aviation, Femi Fani-Kayode, have obviously squashed their beef.

Naija News reports that Dino and Fani Kayode met in Abuja today, November 28, 2022, during the Abubakar Olusola Saraki Memorial Lecture.

Recall that the two Nigerian politicians have been fighting dirty on social media since the 2023 general election campaigns began.

While Dino supports the PDP flagbearer, Atiku Abubakar, FFK supports the All Progressives Congress (APC) presidential candidate, Bola Tinubu.

A video from the event which emerged online shows that the duo have put aside their political differences as they hugged and laughed at the Abuja political gathering.

2023: We Will Fish You Out

Meanwhile, the vice Presidential Candidate of the Labour Party, Sen. Yusuf Datti Ahmed, has disclosed that if elected into office, he and his principal, Peter Obi, will fish out those terrorising the country wherever they are.

Datti said on Monday that the Labour Party presidential candidate would swing into action from the first month in office.

He said the Obi-Datti administration would ensure it stops the incessant killing, kidnapping and stealing of the nation’s resources in the first month of government and begin a healing process for the country.
