July 01, 2024

For singer Eedris Abdulkareem, this festive season is the time to express gratitude to God for all the gifts he has received in life. One of the most lovely of the bountiful blessings he has has are his kids. Hence, the veteran musician could not help but flaunt them.

Eedris Abdulkareem who is well known for his hit song Nigeria Jagajaga took to his Instagram page to show off the adorable children he birthed with his wife Yetunde.

The singer who married his wife after having a long relationship with her was apparently grateful for the lovely kids she produced for him. Together, the couple have two beautiful girls and a lovely boy.

On his Instagram page, Eedris shared several photos of his kids who were obviously having fun being captured together. The adorable images showed them at their best.
