Two men have left social media users in stitches after they were recording taking their unfinished car for a test drive.

It is our Lambo

The two who called their unfinished automobile a Lamborghini were spotted on top of the vehicle with no bodywork and only an iron skeleton.

The video was shared on Instagram by @instablog9ja, and people were concerned about the two guys' safety.

Teenage builds car toys with used material

A teenage boy identified as Ikechukwu Divine Oramife was spotlighted on TUKO.co.ke after amusing netizens with his many toy vehicles built with local wastes like used DVD players.

While flaunting his toy car designs, Ikechukwu revealed that making a replica of the Lamborghini 2021 model took him more than a month.

He explained that he would love to go to school and become an automobile engineer to improve his craft.
