Former queen of Chakacha Princess Faridah has opened up about her salvation for the first time ever.

The entertainer famed for her dancing prowess revealed that she decided to switch from Islam to Christianity while on her way to Kenya from Dubai.

According to the lass who made the revelation in an interview with Milele FM, she gave her life to Jesus aboard a plane.

Faridah said she was on her way from Dubai where she had a performance when God talked to her in a move that saw her get saved without second thought.

The former Chakacha star revealed she was invited to the cockpit during her flight from where she was able to see people in white clothes surrendering their lives to Jesus, and heard God's voice asking her to cross over.

''I had not planned to get saved, but things changed during my flight from Dubai to Kenya. I was invited to the cockpit where I was able to see people in white clothes ahead of us giving their lives to Jesus. It is then when I also heard a voice speaking to me, asking me where will I head when I die,'' she said.''The voice told me that I only have one chance to go to heaven but I had to get saved so as to have it,'' she added.

According to the lass, she immediately approached one of her dancers who was accompanying her and asked him to take her to his church upon touch down at the JKIA.

While in church, she surrendered her life to Jesus and began her journey of salvation.

Faridah said it was at that juncture that she ditched the Islamic faith for Christianity in which she has been in ever since.

The celebrity was one time a name to reckon with in the international entertainment scene having made her name as queen of Chakacha.

TUKO.co.ke understands Faridah is dancer Kanda King's sister and has been working closely with her kin until her salvation.
