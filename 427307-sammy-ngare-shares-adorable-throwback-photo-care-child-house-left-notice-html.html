  • Police officer decided to take over and look after his child
  • Luckily, it was his day off, so there was not much stress for the family

Celebrated cop Sammy Ngare has shared an adorable throwback picture showing him taking on parenting duties after his househelp left without notice.

Ngare took to Facebook to share the pictures saying how he had to step up when his househelp disappeared.

He also noted that taking care of kids is not easy and saluted all mothers who do it.

"On this day in 2011, the house manager decided to leave without notice. I was off duty na ilibidi nifanye kazi zote. (and I had to do all the chores. Kusema ukweli, hii job sio rahisi vile. (To be honest that is not an easy task) Big up all responsible mothers," he posted.
