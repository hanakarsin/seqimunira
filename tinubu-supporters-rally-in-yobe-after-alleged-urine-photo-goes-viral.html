Tinubu Supporters Rally In Yobe After Alleged Urine Photo Goes Viral

February 17, 2024
Post

The group supporting presidential aspirant, Bola Ahmed Tinubu have gone on an enlightenment campaign in Yobe.

The leader of the sensitization group, Ali Rambo said the sensitization was to motivate people to show support for Tinubu’s presidential aspiration.

He said: “If Bola Tinubu had the opportunity for eight years in Lagos and made it the most viable economy in Africa, I want to believe that if he is allowed to run this country as president, he will do better.

“Tinubu has done it before, we try to track his record, we try to look at his profile in terms of performance when it comes to public administration.”

Rambo stated further that the sensitization is to be carried out in 36 states of the federation including the Federal Capital Territory (FCT).

The sensitization is coming after a photograph which showed the politician with a wet attire went viral.

Many people alleged that the wetness is as a result of the passage of urine. However a support group of the chieftian stated that the viral photo was a photoshop to mar the politician presidential 2023 ambition.
