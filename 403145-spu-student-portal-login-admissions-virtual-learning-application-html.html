SPU student portal: login, admissions, virtual learning, application

March 31, 2024
Post

St. Paul’s University (SPU) is one of Kenya's premier higher learning institutions with highly qualified and dedicated staff that offer holistic education. It is a Christian ecumenical institution that provides a comfortable environment for research, study, and growth. The university is among others that have embraced technology and implemented a sophisticated student portal to serve its growing population better. How does the SPU student portal work?

With technology's outburst in contemporary society, no learning institution can afford to ignore it. That is why universities are implementing online platforms to bring services closer to their learners. And one of the most common is the student portal.

What is the SPU student portal?

The SPU student portal is an online platform that helps understudies registered at the institution for various academic programs and prospects to facilitate specific academic and financial actions. It is a strategic move that eliminates the old-school, time-wasting method of physically queuing for services that are remotely accessible.

How to register for the St. Paul’s University student portal

Anyone looking to access the SPU student portal ought to have an SPU number and password that the university provides. Therefore, individuals not affiliated with the learning institution are barred from accessing the portal.

Usually, the account name is the student's registration number written on the space without slashes or spaces.

How to log into the portal

The university has made it easier for active and prospective students to log in to the portal and access various services. One can do it using the following procedure:

  • Visit the official online platform using this link: students.spu.ac.ke. Alternatively, you can visit their official website (spu.ac.ke) and click on the student portal button on the top left section of the page.
  • On the page that follows, you will notice two empty slots, one with the label SPU Number (no slashes or spaces) and the other one below stating password. Enter the required details on the spaces provided.
  • Click the log in button to access the student's portal or exam portal button if you are interested in exam information.

How to reset your password on the student portal

You have probably wondered what happens when you cannot recall your password or other log in details. The SPU student portal has made it easy to regain access via the forgot password feature. And you can regenerate your details using the following steps:

  • Visit the official St. Paul’s University website and click on the student portal link. It will lead you to the log in page.
  • Since you cannot recall your details, click on the forgot password link below the fillable slots and between the log in and exams portal buttons.
  • On the next page, enter your SPU student number and click submit.
  • You will receive a link that will help you to reset your password and regain access to your account.

What services are available at the SPU student portal?

Can you access the St. Paul's university admission letter download from the student portal? Ordinarily, universities create such online avenues to allow students to access services without physically visiting the university premises. So, it is highly likely that one can download their admission letters on top of other things at the SPU student portal.

Financial services at the portal

Students can access the following financial services from the online platform once logged in:

  • Fee statement viewing and printing.
  • Proforma invoice viewing and printing.
  • Fees structure viewing and printing.
  • Receipts access.

Accessibility to such paperwork remotely makes the registration process easier and faster as a student doesn't have to visit the university premises' finance office.

Academics services accessible online

The learning institution has allowed students to access certain academic services they would have done manually at their respective departments. These include:

  • Timetable viewing.
  • Registration for units.
  • Access to the list of registered units.
  • Access to the list of attempted units.
  • Exam card access.
  • Provisional results access.
  • Yearly provisional results access.

The ease of processing such academic paperwork makes learning better and more comfortable by reducing time wastage. Students can also learn about their registered units early and start researching before studies commence.

SPU student e-learning portal and virtual learning portal

St. Paul's University has a virtual and e-learning portal for undergraduate, post-graduate, diploma, certificate, and Ph.D. students.

How to access the vlearning and e-learning portals

Like the student's portal, the vlearning SPU portal, as well as that for e-learning, are accessible to only the university's registered students. Access requires already assigned log in details, and you can access the platform in the following way:
