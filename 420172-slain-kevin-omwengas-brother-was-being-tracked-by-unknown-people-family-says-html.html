The family of Wycliffe Omwenga has revealed that he lived in fear in the days leading to his death on Wednesday, July 21.

Wycliffe was found dead in his Riruta home just months after his elder brother and businessman Kevin Omwenga was shot dead in his Kilimani home in August 2020.

According to a report by the Standard, Wycliffe's family member said he had moved to Riruta to evade some unknown people who were tracking him.

Wycliffe had also said he wanted a quiet place by moving to Riruta since he was a key witness in the murder case of his brother.

"I was with him until Monday then travelled out of the city in the afternoon hours. Afterwards I could not reach him on phone. That day I asked him why he had moved to Riruta and he told me he wanted a quiet place since he was a main witness in our brother’s case”, Ibrahim Onkendi said.

He had lived with Kevin in Kilimani before moving out after the shooting incident.

According to Onkendi, Kevin named Wycliffe as his next of kin and he (Wycliffe) was about to take over some of the properties before his demise.

Additionally, Wycliffe's younger brother said his body had no signs to show struggle before his death.

“There is nothing to show how he died. I have viewed the body and it appears well, the autopsy will reveal the truth,” he said.
