Mark Zuckerberg, wife Priscilla Chan promise to give 99% of their wealth to charity

June 30, 2024
Post

- Facebook's CEO and wife Priscilla Chan, have promised to donate 99% of their wealth to the Chan Zuckerberg Initiative (CZI)

- CZI is seeking to control and eliminate all human diseases and also improve the quality of education around the world

- The donation will go towards providing necessary but expensive tools used by teachers, doctors and scientists to help them achieve their goals

Facebook CEO Mark Zuckerberg and wife Priscilla Chan, have announced that they will donate 99% of their earnings to the Chan Zuckerberg Initiative (CZI).

The CZI is an organisation targeted at using its resources to deal with all human diseases and improve education in the world.
