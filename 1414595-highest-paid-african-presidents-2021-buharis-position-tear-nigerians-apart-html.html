Given that they are public servants, asides from being the chief security officers of their respective countries, African presidents also receive salaries like any other worker.

The pay package which is usually monthly also comes with numerous allowances. According to Answers Africa, the current President of Cameroon Paul Diya is the highest paid African president at the moment.

Paul who has held sway at the country's seat of power since November 1982 receives $620, 976 as pay.

The top 20 list of the highest paid African presidents in 2021 as complied by Africa Info 247 are as follows:

1. Paul Biya, Cameroon - $620,976

2. King Mohammed VI, Morocco - $488,604

3. Cyril Ramaphosa, South Africa - $223,500
