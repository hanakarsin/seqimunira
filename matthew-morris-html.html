MattyB - Age, Family, Bio

June 07, 2024
Post

Born Matthew David Morris, he is a popular rapper whose music has been featured on shows such as The Today Show, The Wendy Williams Show, the Dr. Phil Show, and EXTRA. He has earned over 14 million subscribers and more than 3.1 billion views to his MattyBRaps YouTube channel. He has another 3 million subscribers on his MattyBVlogs YouTube channel.

Before Fame

He played peewee baseball and was beginning school before becoming an internet sensation across multiple social media platforms, including YouTube.

Trivia

Billboard Magazine selected him as a Top 21 Under 21 in 2013 and he has been noticed by A-list celebrities such as Katy Perry, Ryan Seacrest, and Ashton Kutcher. He has his own record label, publishing company, and concert and touring company.

Family Life

He has four siblings, Sarah, Blake Jr., John and Joshua. His parents are Tawny Morris and Blake Morris Sr. 

Associated With

He is an aspiring actor who has been featured in commercials that have appeared on national television. He's appeared alongside Dwayne Johnson on television as well.
