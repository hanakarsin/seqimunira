David Iacono - Age, Family, Bio

June 13, 2024
Post

This former child model, whose mom got him into print ads for J. Crew, Little Levi's, and Century 21, grew into a talented theater, film, and TV actor. His breakthrough came in 2020, with two Netflix series: Grand Army and Social Distance, followed by a role in HBO Max's The Flight Attendant. He uses his Instagram account, where he has 650,000 followers, to let people know about his latest releases, but also to poke fun at himself, recognize others, and generally stay as down to earth as possible. 

Before Fame

He made his film debut at nine in the 2011 thriller Choose. He graduated from Fiorello H. LaGuardia High School of Music and Art and Performing Arts.

Trivia

He appeared on Saturday Night Live in 2014, hosted by Melissa McCarthy, in a sketch where he played a painting come to life. He also had roles in The Good Doctor, Blue Bloods, and NCIS: New Orleans. He's a fan of the Rolling Stones, Bo Burnham, and Jim Carrey.

Family Life

He's of Puerto Rican and Italian descent. His mother worked as a mammography technologist and his dad as a union carpenter. He grew up in Brooklyn.

Associated With

His castmates on Grand Army include Keara Graves, Odley Jean, and Amir Bageria.
