You Must Not Smile At My Man Or Date My Ex

March 14, 2024
Post

Nollywood actress, Nkechi Blessing, has warned her friends on how to act around her partner.

The thespian stated that her friends are not permitted to smile with her man.

She argued that even though she trusts him to be faithful to her, she doesn’t trust her friends.

She further added that her friends aren’t allowed to ask her man for help when they get stranded as she noted how a lady stole her friend’s man, got pregnant for him, and even dared her friend to do her worse.

Nkechi also warned them to stay away from her ex-boyfriends.

She said, “As my friend ,you aren’t permitted to shine your teeth at my mine. He wouldn’t even answer you. To the point you will now say, you’re stranded, can you please go and spend a night in his house, my sister I no go allow you. I trust my man oo but I no trust the nyash wey you carry.

“My gender, I fear una, I fear myself sef. I can’t lose guard like that and again if you are my friend, you think that it’s someone that I have dated before that you want to marry, e don dey enter your eye when we dey date. Thief, you didn’t know how to say it. You know see man again, it is the one I dump that you feel is good. E good na im I leave ma, werey”.
