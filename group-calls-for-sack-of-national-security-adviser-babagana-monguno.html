Group Calls For Sack Of National Security Adviser, Babagana Monguno

May 26, 2024
Post

The Arewa Youth Assembly (AYA), has called for the immediate sack of the National Security Adviser, Babagana Monguno for failing the nation.

The speaker of the group, Mohammed Salihu Danlami in a statement called on President Muhammadu Buhari to relieve Babagana of his duties.

Danlami lamented on the state of the victims of the Abuja-Kaduna train attack pointing to the viral video of the abductees being flogged.

The statement read: “As the victims of the ill-fated Abuja-Kaduna bound train marked 120 days in captivity, it was celebrated by the terrorist through a released video depicting how they (victims) are being molested.”

The group insisted that the non-performance of state actors led to the invasion of Kuje Maximum Prison, Abuja which has led to the release of numerous criminals.

The group said it has observed that the insult to the country’s supremacy wouldn’t have been possible if the National Security Adviser, who is supposed to guide Mr President had not run out of ideas.

“Since his appointment over seven years ago, we strongly believe that the man has run out of steam and does not have anything new to offer Nigerians, hence, the honourable thing for him to do is to pack his luggage and return to his retirement destination, where he fails to, Mr President should urgently sack him and get him replaced,” the group added.
