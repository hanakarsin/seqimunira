Scare as Tanzania airplane suffers multiple bird strikes while landing

June 11, 2024
Post

- The plane encountered the birds and during impact, blood was splattered on its windscreens leaving the pilots with impaired vision

- The birds also got lodged in the plane's engine vents, landing gear compartments and also hit important equipment that control air speed

- Nobody was hurt as pilots managed to maneuver the plane safely to Mwanza Airport though at a precarious state

Passengers traveling aboard a Tanzanian plane on the morning of Sunday, December 9, escaped death by a whisker after their aircraft flew into a flock of birds.

TUKO.co.ke learnt that the carrier owned, by Precision Air was headed to Mwanza Airport from Dar-es Salaam before it was caught up in multiple bird strikes.

In a statement, Aviation Herald said visibility from the plane's cockpit was reduced by blood from the birds which was splattered on the windscreens after impact.

After landing in Mwanza, a post flight inspection was conducted and it indicated the incident had capacity to cause an accident.
