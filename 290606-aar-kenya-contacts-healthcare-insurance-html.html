  • Outpatient services: Advice concerning nutritional matters, vaccination of children as per the guideline issued, cancer tests, treatments and consultation, ophthalmic services, prenatal and postnatal care, surgery of the gums and city scans.

3. Work injury

A financial compensation is made when one cannot withstand body injuries that have to be triggered by violence, accidents or any other observable means that you select.

4. Doctors indemnity

This insurance covers liabilities that arise from the carelessness of doctors when on duty. This negligence can result in deaths, injuries, and shock.

5. Homeowners insurance coverage

This insurance covers occupants of a residential building or even home proprietors from losses that may be caused by fires, storms that can lead to trees falling down or even striking the house, the act of nature such as earthquakes or even strikes.

This cover also protects an individual from damages of personal items such as iPads, light emitting diodes televisions, jewels as well as digital single lens cameras.
