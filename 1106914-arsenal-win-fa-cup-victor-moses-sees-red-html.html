Arsenal win FA Cup as Victor Moses sees red

August 03, 2024
Post

Arsenal have won the FA Cup after defeating ten-man Chelsea 2 - 1 in the final played at the Wembley stadium.

Aaron Ramsey scored the winner after Alexis Sanchez controversial opener was cancelled out by Diego Costa with the Blues already one man down at the time.

Sanchez scored a controversial opener when he first handled the ball and followed it up where Ramsey interfered with play from an offside position.
